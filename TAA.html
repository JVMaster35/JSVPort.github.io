About Project

As a part-time programmer for Armada Studios, I contributed to the development of their modern, fan-sequel to Sega's 1995 Tails Adventure game, dubbed Tails Adventure Armada, where the iconic hero once again does battle with the sinister Battle Bird Armada.

Following the tradition that the original, Tails Adventure Armada is a platform game, with 3d modelling and controls, akin to a free-roaming game. In keeping with the elements of its original counterpart, the game will involve utilizing various gadgets to remove obstacles, battle enemies, and access new areas, while also playing to the title-character's ability to fly to traverse terrian, ascend platforms and avoid any unwanted attention.

As one of the developers, I am charged with helping bringing the game to life utilizing C# coding and 3d art models made by the artists. Part of my involvment included some of the game controller components for Tails, including movement, jumping, flying, animation synchronization, and enemy detection and combat.

The project itself is still in the development stage, but I'm hoping that my contributions to its development will help it stand out amongst Sonic fan-games.
